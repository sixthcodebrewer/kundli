Project 1: Contract Management App

A person works in a company and does not have much resources and knowledge to look for all the obligations in a company’s contracts. This obligation contains clauses that could lead to a compliance violation.

Our solution is to create software that helps the company to easily find the obligations which will lead to avoid an audit by showing them rights, restrictions, exposure, and ways to fix them, hence help them avoid compliance fees and reducing administrative burden.
